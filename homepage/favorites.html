My Favorites

My favorite Manga

My favorite manga would have to be a series called Tokyo Ghoul. The intense thriller, psychological, dark fantasy is a very interesting story about a young boy who becomes half ghoul and half human. Trying to make peace between both races he finds himself trapped with both worlds and blames himself for everything but never gives up. I loved every second of it that it had me binge reading almost every night.

My favorite Car

My favorite kind of car is a Camaro, which I have one of my own. It is lots of fun driving it every day and I wouldn't trade it for the world. My Camaro is a 2014 LT 3.6L and I would soon like to upgrade to an SS and possibly track it every chance I get.

My Favorite Game

My favorite game of all time is a game called Halo Reach. I have a lot of good memories during my childhood playing this game with my friends and my brother. We would hop on after school every day and play till night and would sometimes forget about our homework.
